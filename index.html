Gym-Based Workouts

What it is: Structured exercises using equipment like dumbbells, barbells, machines, and free weights.

How to get fit:

  • Build muscle strength and endurance
  • Progressive overload (gradually increase weight)
  • Target specific muscle groups
  • Follow structured split routines (chest day, leg day, etc.)

Benefits for teens:

  • Builds confidence and self-esteem
  • Supervised environment with trainers
  • Measurable progress through weight tracking
  • Social community and motivation

Running & Cardio Training

What it is: High-intensity aerobic exercises like running, jogging, cycling, or HIIT to boost heart health.

How to get fit:

  • Improve cardiovascular health (heart & lungs)
  • Burn calories for weight loss
  • Increase stamina and endurance
  • Use interval training for maximum efficiency

Benefits for teens:

  • Boosts mental health and mood
  • Improves focus and concentration
  • Stress relief from daily pressures
  • No gym membership needed

Outdoor Activities

What it is: Nature-based fitness like hiking, cycling, trail running, and outdoor trekking.

How to get fit:

  • Build leg strength and endurance (especially hiking)
  • Improve cardiovascular health
  • Low-impact on joints (compared to pavement)
  • Adapt difficulty based on terrain

Benefits for teens:

  • Connect with nature and fresh air
  • Improve mental wellbeing and mood
  • Adventure and exploration aspect
  • Free or low-cost activity

  • Excellent for cardiovascular fitness
